Yuki, Yami, & Yang

My three wonderful cats and their lives together.

A view into these cats' lives:

  • March 5th, 2021 - Yuki was born in the comfort of his foster home with Our Farm Santuary. He has two brothers and one sister that have been adopted.
  • July 11th, 2021 - I adopted Yuki from Our Farm Sanctuary and took him home.
  • November 12, 2021 - Yami showed up on our doorstep. She was small and sickly, so we took her in and started caring for her.
  • December 4th, 2021 - Yang appeared outside our door begging for food. We said hello, fed him, and made plans to TNR (Trap, Neuter, Release) him.
  • December 28th, 2021 - Yang showed up limping and with a cut on his back right leg. We took him inside and scheduled a vet appointment. Little did we realize, he would be a permanent family member.
  • January 28th, 2022 - Both Yami and Yang got their spay/neuter surgeries. After healing, they can finally interact and play with each other!
  • September 1st, 2023 - Official move in day to a bigger house, with stairs! The cats explore and settle in. They love chasing each other up and down the stairs. Yami is a bit more nervous about the move than the boys.
